About Us

At Consilio Housing, we believe that a well-designed home is the foundation for a happy and fulfilling life. We are a team of passionate architects and designers dedicated to creating spaces that not only look beautiful, but also function seamlessly with your lifestyle. Beyond aesthetics, we prioritize functionality and sustainability in all of our designs. We believe that a home should not only look good, but also work for you and your family, whether that means maximizing storage space, optimizing natural light, or incorporating eco-friendly materials. At Consilio Housing, we are committed to providing exceptional service and a seamless experience from start to finish. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and our ability to bring your dream home to life on time and on budget
